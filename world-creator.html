World Overview

Start here — these answers shape everything else:

  • What is the single most unique thing about this world that no other world has?
  • What is the central tension or conflict that defines life in this world?
  • What tone does this world have — hopeful, grim, whimsical, brutal, mysterious?
  • Is magic/technology abundant or rare? Does it make life better or worse?
  • What do ordinary people fear most in this world?
  • What do ordinary people hope for most?
  • What's something that seems normal here that would horrify an outsider?
🗺️

Geography

Building the physical world:

  • How does the landscape shape the people who live there? (mountains = isolation, trade routes = culture mixing)
  • Are there places that are forbidden, cursed, or considered sacred?
  • What natural resources exist and who controls them?
  • Are there places that are physically dangerous just to be in?
  • How do people travel between regions — and who can afford to?
  • Has the geography changed due to history, magic, or disaster?
📜

History & Timeline

Building a history that matters:

  • What event, if reversed, would make this world completely unrecognizable?
  • Whose version of history gets told? Whose is suppressed or forgotten?
  • What mistake from the past is the world still paying for today?
  • Is there a "before" — a golden age, a lost civilization, an older world?
  • What catastrophe shaped the current era? War, plague, magical disaster, god-death?
  • What do common people actually know about history vs. what's true?
✨

Magic / Power System

Designing a compelling magic system:

  • What are the rules? A magic system with clear limits is more interesting than one with none.
  • What does it cost? (exhaustion, blood, years of life, sanity, emotion)
  • Is magic feared, worshipped, or treated as mundane?
  • Who has access — is it innate, learned, or granted by an outside force?
  • What happens when magic is misused or overused?
  • Is there a "dark" side — forbidden magic, corruption, things that shouldn't be done?
  • How does magic interact with technology or religion?
⚙️

Technology

  • What is the general tech level? (stone age, medieval, industrial, modern, post-singularity)
  • Is technology evenly distributed or does only one group/region have advanced tech?
  • Does magic replace technology, coexist with it, or conflict with it?
  • What's the most transformative technology in this world?
  • Is there tech that's been lost or destroyed? Why?
  • Who controls the most powerful technology — and who is denied it?
⚖️

Economy & Society

  • What does wealth look like here — gold, land, magic, information, loyalty?
  • How easy is it for someone to move between social classes?
  • What rights do different groups have — based on species, gender, magic ability, birth?
  • What is the justice system? Who enforces laws, and are they just?
  • What does the average person's day look like?
  • What do people trade internationally, and who profits most?
🏛️

Factions & Politics

  • Who holds real power vs. ceremonial power?
  • What does each faction actually want, and what will they sacrifice for it?
  • Which factions appear to be allies but secretly hate each other?
  • Is there a faction that everyone underestimates?
  • Who are the kingmakers — the ones who don't rule but decide who does?
  • What would cause two opposing factions to briefly unite?
🧬

Races & Species

  • What is the history between different races — ancient alliances, old wars, cultural theft?
  • Are there racial stereotypes in this world, and are they accurate or propaganda?
  • What does each race value most? What do they fear?
  • Are there mixed-race individuals and how are they treated?
  • Which species is most dominant politically, and is that recent or ancient?
  • Are there species considered "lesser" or "other" by the dominant culture?
🔱

Religion & Mythology

  • Are the gods real and provable, or a matter of faith?
  • Do gods intervene directly, or work through chosen followers?
  • What happens to people when they die? Is there an afterlife, reincarnation, or oblivion?
  • Is there religious conflict — between faiths, or between religion and state?
  • What is considered blasphemy? What's sacred that others would call mundane?
  • Are there gods who were killed, forgotten, or turned evil?
🎭

Culture & Daily Life

  • What art form is most valued in this world — music, storytelling, visual art, architecture?
  • What do people do for fun? What brings communities together?
  • What is considered the height of rudeness or the greatest honor?
  • How is death mourned or celebrated?
  • What foods are common, and which are luxury items only the wealthy enjoy?
  • Are there cultural taboos that outsiders would find baffling?
  • How does language differ between regions, classes, or species?
📍

Key Locations

  • What does this place smell like, sound like, feel like?
  • Who lives here and who is kept out?
  • What has happened here that left a mark — physically, spiritually, historically?
  • Is this place what it appears to be, or is there a hidden layer?
  • What would someone never expect about this place?

Notable Figures

World-level important characters — rulers, legends, villains, historical figures. For chatbot characters, use the Character Creator.

Conflicts & Threats

  • What's the conflict everyone knows about vs. the one no one talks about?
  • Who benefits from the current conflict continuing — who has a stake in it never ending?
  • Is the main threat external (invasion, monster, plague) or internal (corruption, civil war)?
  • What would have to happen for this conflict to truly end?
  • What does the antagonist believe they're doing right?
  • Is there a threat that no one has recognized yet?
🔐

Secrets & Hidden Lore

  • What truth would shatter the world's foundation if revealed?
  • Who knows the real history — and what are they doing with that knowledge?
  • Is there something that appears benevolent but is actually sinister?
  • What has been deliberately erased from history, and by whom?
  • What does the protagonist think is true that isn't?
